Interactive Mass-Splitting Example

Split-mass transport with two sources

Source $A$ has $2\text{ kg}$ of loose dirt, and source $B$ has $1\text{ kg}$. Hole $C$ needs $1\text{ kg}$, while hole $D$ needs $2\text{ kg}$. Once you choose how much mass to send from $A$ to $C$, conservation determines the other three entries of the transport plan automatically.
