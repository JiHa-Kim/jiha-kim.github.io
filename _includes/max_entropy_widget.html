Iterative Entropy Maximization

Geometrically, simulating a diffusion process (heat flow) is equivalent to taking gradient ascent steps to maximize differential entropy.

Press play or scrub the slider to run the optimization algorithm on an arbitrary starting distribution. Notice how the distribution iteratively smooths out—strictly increasing its entropy \( H \)—until it perfectly converges to the most unbiased theoretical state (Uniform or Gaussian) dictated by its constraints.
