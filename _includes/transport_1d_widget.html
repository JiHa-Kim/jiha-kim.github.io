Inverse Transform Sampling

Drag the \( u \) slider along the horizontal axis, which represents uniform noise. The solid blue curve is the Inverse CDF \( x = F^{-1}(u) \). The shape along the vertical axis shows the target density \( p(x) \). Notice how the cumulative mass (the filled orange area) visually grows in proportion to your chosen \( u \) input!

\( x \) = 4.23
