Drag the three source points $x_1,x_2,x_3$. The middle panel shows $T(x_i)$, and the right panel asks whether the same triple can be explained by one convex potential $\phi$.

Cycle Test
$\displaystyle \sum_{i=1}^3 \langle x_i,T(x_i)\rangle \ge \sum_{i=1}^3 \langle x_i,T(x_{i+1})\rangle$
Negative means the cyclic reassignment is cheaper than the original pairing.
Support Test
$\displaystyle \phi(x_j)\ge s_i(x_j), \qquad s_i(x_j)=\phi(x_i)+\langle T(x_i),x_j-x_i\rangle$
Colored lines are the affine supports $s_i(x_j)$ and black dots are the witness heights $\phi(x_j)$. The red circle marks the first violated support.
