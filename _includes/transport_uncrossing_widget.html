Uncrossed versus crossed on one number line

Drag the points themselves on the number line. The widget keeps the order constraints $x_1 < x_2$ and $y_1 < y_2$. The top row shows the uncrossed matching, and the bottom row shows the crossed matching.

Orange points are sources and blue points are targets. Within each color, the left point is subscript $1$ and the right point is subscript $2$. Dragging changes the actual 1D distances, so every segment is horizontal.
