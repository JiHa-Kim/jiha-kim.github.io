Example

Two sealed $1\text{ kg}$ dirt bags

Source $A$ and source $B$ each have one sealed $1\text{ kg}$ bag of dirt. Destination $C$ and destination $D$ each need $1\text{ kg}$. Since a bag cannot be torn open, each bag must go whole to exactly one destination.

Shipping Cost per Bag
From → To

To $D$
From $A$
$\$1$

From $B$
$\$3$

There are only two feasible plans. The uncrossed plan costs $\$3$, while the crossed plan costs $\$7$, so Plan $1$ is optimal.
